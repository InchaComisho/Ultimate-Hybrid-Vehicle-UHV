# Contributing

Contributions are welcome. This repository is an open-invention concept, so useful contributions may include simulations, translations, diagrams, safety review, prototype reports, field-test notes, and clearer technical explanations.

Please keep the tone academic, technical, and accessible.

## Contribution Guidelines

* Avoid claims of perpetual motion, unlimited energy, self-sustaining propulsion, or main-drive power from vehicle-generated airflow.
* Distinguish validated results from conceptual proposals.
* Use careful wording such as "concept," "proposal," "may," "could," and "requires validation" when evidence is not yet available.
* Include test conditions, units, assumptions, and limitations when submitting simulations or prototype reports.
* Consider road safety, visibility, water use, wet-road risk, electrical waterproofing, hygiene, and local transport regulations.
* Preserve the open-invention philosophy and provide attribution where possible.

## Suggested Contribution Areas

* improved evaporative cooling models
* aerodynamic drag and auxiliary wind recovery analysis
* Center-Mist Cooling prototype diagrams
* dry-climate field test plans
* bus, delivery vehicle, airport, port, railway, or ship retrofit studies
* English, Japanese, Arabic, and other language documentation
* regulatory and safety checklists
